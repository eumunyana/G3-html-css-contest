Discover LocalGuide

Welcome to LocalGuide, your go-to concierge service for exploring the best of Rwanda. Whether you are a local, ex-pat, or tourist, we are here to help you find the perfect travel and entertainment options to make the most of your time. With LocalGuide, you can easily discover and book a wide range of services, from travel arrangements to exciting experiences. Start your journey with us to day!

Wildlife Safaris

Embark on an unforgettable wildlife safari and witness Rwanda's incredible biodiversity. Discover the country's national parks, encounter majestic animals, and marvel at the breathtaking landscapes under the guidance of experienced wildlife experts.

Cultural Experiences

Immerse yourself in Rwanda's vibrant culture and traditions through immersive experiences. Engage in traditional dance performances, visit local artisans, and participate in community activities to gain a deeper understanding of the country's heritage.

Adventure Sports

Get your adrenaline pumping with a range of adventure sports in Rwanda. Go hiking through lush forests, embark on thrilling mountain biking trails, or challenge yourself with exhilarating water sports on the lakes,ponds and rivers.
